Grading And Homework Policy



Grading and Homework Policy: The students in this program are expected to complete all assingments even if that
requires them to complete assignments at home. A student's grade will consist of 55% Practical 20% Test/Exams, 20%
Assignments/Classwork, and 5% Portfolio. each assignment in each area (Pratical, Test/Exams,
Assignments/Classwork, and Portfolio)
