Student Related Information/Credits

Statement of Equal Treatment: The instructor and students in this program will act with integrity and strive to engage in
equitable verbal and non-verbal behaviour regardless of differences in race, gender, abilities, or religion.

Students with Disabillities: Any student who has a documented disability and is in need of academic accommodations
should notify the instructor of this program and/or contact the Special Education Consultant (845-291-0300).
Accommodations are individualized and in accordance wih the Individuals with Disabillities Act, Section 504 of the
Regulations Act of 1973 and the Americans with Disabilities Act of 1992, and Article 89 of the New York State Education
Law and part 200 of the commissioner's regulations.

Student Support: Students having difficulty with the program are encouraged to approach the instructor and/or guidance
counselor in order to arrange extra help as needed

Available Credits: See instructor and/or Guidance Counselor
