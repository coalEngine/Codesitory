Video Game Review: The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild


Description:

Breath of the Wild, came out on March 3,2017. it is a Open World game, that some critics say is a landmark for the genre. the game offers multiple solutions to puzzle instead of one. The game host 4 different dungeons, as well as Mini Bosses as you travel throughout the world.
This game also has lots of side content likethe Korok seeds, the side quest, cooking, minigames.

Story:

The story follows a boy named Link as he fights to regan his memories and rescue his trapped princess from a evil being called "Ganon". After losing 100 years ago to "Ganon" link is resurrected and awoken by Princess Zelda with none of his memories, he sets out to regan his lost power

The Good:


  • Great combat
  • Replayablillty
  • Massive World
  • Interesting side quest
  • Really great art style

The Bad:

  • Weapons break
  • Non traditional dungeons
  • Scales enemies based on location
  • Easiest bosses in the entire series


Conclusion/Rating:


I'll give this game a 9 out 10, it may have its faults but overall its one of the best games on the market.
