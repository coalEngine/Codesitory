						 Program Description: Students will become familiar with various computer applications and computer programming 
 						languages while developing troubleshooting techniques. This program prepares students to become part of this rapidly 
 						expanding field and to enter higher education. Students may participate in an internship or clinical experience. Students

  						participate in several Robotics Competitions and Video Game Competitions.
